Entrepreneurship and App Development program for school students



We’re building young innovators in India.

We teach coding, app development, and other creative technologies to primary, middle, and senior school students across the nation during vacations and weekends. One camp at a time, we hope to inspire the next generation of innovators and tech entrepreneurs in the country.

Don’t just play games — make them.

We know you love technology. You listen to music, play games online and on your phones, and browse websites all day long. At Camp K12, we take your relationship with Technology to a whole new level by transforming you into a creator rather than a user of these things.Don’t just play games, make them. Don’t just listen to Avicci and Hardwell, make your own EDM. Make your own movies, animations, designs, and mobile apps, because you can, and because it’s fun to be able to turn your ideas into reality. Come build with us.

Start young and build often.

Some say it’s “never too late” to start, but at Camp K12 we believe it’s never too early. We chose to build our courses for KG – 12 students (hence the K12 in our name) rather than colleges because frankly, college is too late. Our goal is to work with impressionable young minds and to inculcate in them the healthy habits of problem solving and creativity. We want students in Indian schools to develop a sense of confidence in their abilities to create and an addiction to innovation — a muscle memory for building apps, movies, music, animations, graphics, and games.We feel that it is this early exposure and positive habits that will allow our campers in India to be the makers, doers, and entrepreneurs of tomorrow.

Learn by doing — our curriculum and approach

Our curriculum was designed by our founder during his five years at MIT (Massachusetts Institute of Technology, USA) and has been implemented successfully in academic institutions across the world, including in Italy, Brazil, Kenya, China, and the United States. Campers complete multiple exciting projects right from day one, and work their way up to a Final Project which they showcase to their parents, teachers, and school principal on the final day of the camp.Our campers work on projects for causes and organizations they are passionate about or that solve real-world problems. Examples include Android Apps for the Disabled, Games to teach a new language to a child, Cards for a family member’s birthday or festival, TV advertisements about Android vs. iOS platforms, and Websites for their school clubs.

Big online competitions throughout the year

We’re all about giving you the chance to make stuff. So when we’re not running our camps at schools, we run creative technology competitions online. No matter where you are in the country, you can sign up and put your creativity and skills to the test against your classmates all over nation.Our 2015 Competition was run in collaboration with Adobe India, and included themes such as “Design iPhone 10”, “Create an Android game with Flying Objects”, create a “TV ad for iOS vs Android”, and “Design a website for the imaginary celebrity Yo! Yo! Money Singh”. Check out the competition recap video to see what the winners created for these themes.
